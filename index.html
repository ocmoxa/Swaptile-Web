SwapTile

The puzzle-game for assembling images.

Every day, we spend most of our time on the bus or subway. We often surf social media, listen to music or think about something. Life goes by very quickly. There is a lot of stress and anxiety. So we created the game SwapTile - a swap puzzle. The game is simple, but it helps you to relax and train your brain. Instead of 15 minutes of social media, now we can complete a beautiful picture and forget about the life pressure.

The main aim of the game is to find the original position of the puzzle pieces. The pieces shuffled before starting. We can swap the locations of any two pieces. A tip or a preview can help to assemble the original image.

The game is simple, but it includes a volume gallery of beautiful, vivid, and artistic pictures. New pictures every day! We guarantee that you will be interested and not bored. When you choose an image, by the next step, you need to select the number of pieces and then start playing! You can continue the unfinished pictures at any time (both online or offline). It's just amazing, isn't it? The game is free. The paintings are also free to download and use. We sincerely thank the Unsplash website for allowing us to use the images. So if you love some pictures, you can press the "share" button to share with your friends or download the image!

Our team spent a lot of time writing the application and testing it. We hope you will have a good experience using SwapTile.

Please, enjoy the game!
